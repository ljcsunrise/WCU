Admissions

We do not define you by exams.

WCU evaluates applicants through potential, portfolio evidence, and contribution mindset, not test scores alone.

Why WCU

  • Research opportunities from first year.
  • Project portfolio over rote memorization.
  • Global cohort in a highly focused campus setting.

Application Process

  1. Submit academic history + personal statement.
  2. Upload project portfolio or research sample.
  3. Interview with faculty panel.
  4. Receive decision and scholarship pathway.

Admission Principle

Applicants are assessed for depth of thought, technical curiosity, and ability to learn in ambiguous conditions. Scores may inform context, but they do not define merit.
